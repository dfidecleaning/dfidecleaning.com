FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a cleaning cost?

Standard Cleaning starts at $180 and Deep Cleaning starts at $260. Final price depends on your home size and any add-ons you choose. You'll see the exact price before paying — no surprises.

What products do you use?

We exclusively use Branch Basics, Dr. Bronner's Sal Suds, and Benefect Botanical Disinfectant — all eco-friendly, non-toxic, and safe for children and pets.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No — many of our clients provide access and we handle everything. You'll receive a confirmation when we arrive and when we're done.

What is your satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not 100% satisfied, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the areas of concern at no additional charge.

How do I book a cleaning?

You can book online through our booking system, send us a WhatsApp message, or fill out our contact form. We respond within 1 hour during business hours.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes — we bring all cleaning products and equipment. You don't need to provide anything unless you have a specific product preference.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers through our secure payment portal. Payment is processed after each service.

What does the Tidy-Up service include?

Our Heavy Tidy-Up covers organizing visible areas — sorting items, clearing surfaces, and resetting rooms to an orderly state. We never discard any belongings without your explicit permission. Photos of the areas are recommended before booking. Additional fees may apply for excessive clutter.

What is your cancellation policy?

A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
